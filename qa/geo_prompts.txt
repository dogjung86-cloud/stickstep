[15] Hornblende/amphibole specimen (각섬석): elongated dark greenish-black prismatic crystal, dull luster, columnar shape.

[16] Calcite specimen (방해석): transparent rhombohedral crystal block (tilted box shape), colorless with visible internal cleavage planes.

[17] Magnetite specimen (자철석): dark metallic black octahedral crystals, slightly rusty patches, with 3-4 small steel paper clips clinging to its surface showing magnetism. (클립이 붙은 모습 필수 — 자성 표현)
